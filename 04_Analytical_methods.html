Description#

In this notebook, bounds and approximations for the isotropic effetive stiffness \(\mathbb{C}^*\) of isotropic 2-phase linear elastic materials will be presented. Static and interactive content is offered to visualize and explore the behavior of the presented bounds and approximations.

The content and notation follows the corresponding tutorial of the course. An isotropic stiffness is represented as \(\mathbb{C} = \mathrm{iso}(c_1,c_2) = c_1 \mathbb{P}_1 + c_2 \mathbb{P}_2\) and has the eigenvalues \(c_1 = 3 K\) and \(c_2 = 2 G\), where \(K\) and \(G\) correspond to the compression and shear modulus of the material. The volume fraction of material 2 is denoted as \(f^{(2)} \in [0,1]\).
